Forbes Releases 2016 Midas List Featuring 100 Top VCs

analysis
Published on March 24, 2016March 25, 2016By FinSMEs

As every year, Forbes has released the 2016 Midas List including the 100 top tech venture capital investors.

The podium is comprised of #1 Jim Goetz, partner at Sequoia Capital, followed by #2 Steve Anderson, the founder of Baseline Ventures, #3 Chris Sacca, Founder and Chairman at Lowercase Capital.
The 4th position is held by Peter Fenton, General Partner at Benchmark, while Mary Meeker, Partner at KPCB, is the 5th in the list, the best ranked woman.

Beyond Meeker, the list includes other four women: Jenny Lee, Rebecca Lynn, Theresia Gouw and Beth Seidenberg.
The ranking is also comprised of eleven newcomers: JP Gan, Eric Paley, Bill Trenchard, Allen Zhu, Jeff Clavier, Ben Holmes, Peter Levine, Jeffrey Lieberman, Bob Xu, Dave McClure and Joe Lonsdale.

The most represented firms are: Accel (7), Sequoia Capital (5), Kleiner Perkins Caufield & Byers (4), Andreessen Horowitz (4), Benchmark (4), Founders Fund (3), Sequoia Capital China (3), New Enterprise Associates (3), IVP (2), GGV Capital (2), Index Ventures (2), Orbimed (2), Insight Venture Partners (2), Bain Capital Ventures (2), Norwest Venture Partners (2), Greylock Partners (2), and CRV (2).

Considering the citizenship, the list includes 86 US citizens, 6 chinese, a Swiss, a Russian, a British, a South African, a Canadian, an Australian, a Singaporean, and a Hong Kong citizen.
